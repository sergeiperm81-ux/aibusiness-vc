## Layer 1: The Brain — ChatGPT Plus ($20/month)

ChatGPT Plus replaces your research analyst, strategy consultant, and general assistant. What it handles:

**Market research:** Ask it to analyze competitors, summarize industry reports, identify market trends. What used to take a research assistant 2 days takes 10 minutes.

**Strategy planning:** Business model analysis, pricing strategy, go-to-market planning. Not perfect — but 80% of what a $200/hour consultant delivers.

**Email drafting:** Client communications, partnership outreach, vendor negotiations. It writes professional emails in your voice after 3-4 examples.

**Data analysis:** Upload spreadsheets, ask for insights. It finds patterns a human analyst would spend hours identifying.

**ROI calculation:** $20/month for unlimited analysis across all these functions. A human research analyst costs $4,000-5,000/month. You save $4,980/month.

## Layer 2: The Builder — Cursor Pro ($20/month)

Cursor Pro replaces a junior-to-mid developer for building and maintaining your digital products. Capabilities:

**Website development:** Build a full Next.js site with database integration in hours, not weeks. Cursor's AI writes 80% of the code while you direct the architecture.

**API integrations:** Connect payment processors, email services, analytics. Tasks that would take a developer 2-3 days complete in 2-3 hours.

**Bug fixing:** Paste an error, get a fix. Cursor understands your entire codebase and suggests contextual solutions.

**Database design:** Schema creation, migration scripts, query optimization. It handles the technical heavy lifting while you focus on business logic.

**ROI calculation:** $20/month replaces $6,000-7,500/month developer salary. You save $6,980/month. Even if you ship 70% slower than a dedicated dev, you ship 10x faster than if you had no developer at all.

## Layer 3: The Writer — Jasper Creator ($49/month)

Jasper replaces your content writer and copywriter. What it produces:

**Blog posts:** 10-15 quality blog posts per month with your brand voice trained in. A freelance writer charges $200-500 per post. Jasper produces comparable quality for a flat $49/month.

**Social media:** 30+ LinkedIn posts, 60+ Twitter posts, 30+ Instagram captions per month. A social media manager produces the same volume for $3,000-4,000/month.

**Ad copy:** Google Ads, Facebook Ads, email subject lines. Jasper generates 50+ variations in minutes. An ad copywriter charges $500-2,000 per campaign.

**Sales pages:** Landing pages, product descriptions, email sequences. What takes a copywriter 1-2 weeks takes Jasper 2-3 hours with your editing.

**ROI calculation:** $49/month replaces $4,000-6,000/month in freelance writing costs. You save $5,951/month.

## Layer 4: The Designer — Canva Pro ($13/month)

Canva Pro with AI features replaces your graphic designer. Deliverables:

**Social media graphics:** On-brand templates for every platform. Create 30+ graphics per month in the time it takes to brief a designer on one project.

**Presentations:** Client decks, pitch decks, internal presentations. Professional quality without InDesign or Figma skills.

**Marketing materials:** Brochures, flyers, infographics, email headers. The AI generates initial designs that you customize.

**ROI calculation:** $13/month replaces $3,500-5,000/month designer salary. You save $4,987/month.

## Layer 5: The Automator — Make.com (Free tier)

Make.com replaces your operations assistant. Automations include:

**Lead management:** When a form is submitted, automatically add to CRM, send welcome email, notify you on Slack, and schedule follow-up.

**Content distribution:** Publish one blog post and automatically share it across 5 platforms with platform-specific formatting.

**Invoice processing:** When payment is received, automatically send receipt, update spreadsheet, and trigger onboarding sequence.

**Customer onboarding:** Automated welcome sequences, resource delivery, and check-in scheduling.

**ROI calculation:** $0/month (free tier handles 1,000 operations/month). A virtual assistant doing these tasks costs $2,000-3,000/month. Pure savings.

## Layer 6: The Listener — Otter.ai (Free tier)

Otter.ai replaces meeting transcription and note-taking. Uses:

**Meeting transcription:** Automatic, real-time transcription of all calls. Search transcripts by keyword later.

**Action item extraction:** AI identifies action items and decisions from meeting transcripts.

**Summary generation:** 30-minute meeting condensed into 5-bullet summary automatically.

**ROI calculation:** $0/month. A transcription service charges $1-3 per minute of audio. 10 hours of meetings per month = $600-1,800 saved.

## What You Still Cannot Automate

Be honest about AI limitations in 2026:

1. **Strategic judgment** — AI helps you analyze, but you make the final call on business direction
2. **Client relationships** — The human touch in sales and client management still matters
3. **Quality control** — Everything AI produces needs your review before it ships
4. **Creative direction** — AI executes, but you decide the brand vision and creative strategy
5. **Legal and financial decisions** — Always verify with professionals for contracts, taxes, and compliance

The solopreneur model works because you handle these 5 things — the irreducibly human parts — while AI handles the other 95% of operational work.
