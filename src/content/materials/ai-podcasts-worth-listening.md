# 10 Best AI Podcasts Worth Your Time in 2026

Hundreds of AI podcasts launched in the past two years. Most of them are recycled news headlines with mediocre commentary. These 10 consistently deliver real insight — whether you want to understand the tech, build an AI business, or think seriously about where all of this is heading.

## For Understanding AI Technology

### 1. Lex Fridman Podcast
**Host:** Lex Fridman (MIT researcher)
**Episode length:** 2-4 hours
**Frequency:** 1-2 per week
**Best for:** Deep conversations with AI researchers, founders, and thinkers. Guests include Sam Altman, Dario Amodei, Yann LeCun, and Elon Musk.
**Why listen:** These are 3-hour conversations where guests actually get to finish a thought. Lex does not interrupt. Not for your quick commute — for when you want to go deep.

### 2. The AI Podcast (NVIDIA)
**Host:** Noah Kravitz
**Episode length:** 20-40 minutes
**Frequency:** Weekly
**Best for:** Practical AI applications across industries. How companies actually use AI in production.
**Why listen:** Less hype, more substance. Focused on real implementations, not theoretical possibilities.

### 3. Practical AI (Changelog)
**Host:** Chris Benson and Daniel Whitenack
**Episode length:** 45-60 minutes
**Frequency:** Weekly
**Best for:** Engineers and developers building AI systems. Technical but accessible.
**Why listen:** Bridges the gap between research papers and production deployment. Ideal for practitioners.

## For AI Business

### 4. Hard Fork (NYT)
**Host:** Kevin Roose and Casey Newton
**Episode length:** 45-60 minutes
**Frequency:** Weekly
**Best for:** Tech industry news with sharp AI coverage. Business implications of every major AI development.
**Why listen:** The most fun AI-adjacent pod. Roose and Newton genuinely enjoy arguing with each other, and you learn something every episode.

### 5. AI Breakdown
**Host:** Nathaniel Whittemore
**Episode length:** 15-30 minutes
**Frequency:** Daily
**Best for:** Staying current on AI news without spending hours. Quick, digestible daily updates.
**Why listen:** Only daily AI pod worth subscribing to. You get the important stuff in 15 minutes while driving to work.

### 6. The Cognitive Revolution
**Host:** Nathan Labenz
**Episode length:** 60-90 minutes
**Frequency:** 2 per week
**Best for:** AI strategy, product development, and business applications.
**Why listen:** Nathan actually built AI products — he is not just commenting from the sidelines. His questions hit different because he has lived through the problems.

## For AI and Society

### 7. Eye on AI
**Host:** Craig S. Smith
**Episode length:** 30-45 minutes
**Frequency:** Weekly
**Best for:** AI's impact on society, economy, and humanity. Thoughtful conversations with researchers and policymakers.
**Why listen:** The most consistently thoughtful podcast about AI's broader implications.

### 8. Your Undivided Attention (Center for Humane Technology)
**Host:** Tristan Harris and Aza Raskin
**Episode length:** 45-60 minutes
**Frequency:** Biweekly
**Best for:** AI ethics, safety, and societal impact. Critical perspective on AI development.
**Why listen:** Counterbalance to the AI hype cycle. These are the people asking the hard questions.

## For AI Makers and Builders

### 9. Latent Space
**Host:** Alessio Fanelli and Swyx
**Episode length:** 60-90 minutes
**Frequency:** Weekly
**Best for:** AI engineers and builders. Deep technical discussions on LLMs, agents, and infrastructure.
**Why listen:** The most technically rigorous AI pod out there. If you are building AI products, you should be listening to this every week.

### 10. Last Week in AI
**Host:** Andrey Kurenkov
**Episode length:** 45-60 minutes
**Frequency:** Weekly
**Best for:** Comprehensive weekly roundup of everything that happened in AI.
**Why listen:** If you only have time for one AI podcast per week, this covers everything important.

## How to Pick

| If you want... | Listen to... |
|----------------|-------------|
| Deep understanding | Lex Fridman, Latent Space |
| Daily news | AI Breakdown |
| Weekly roundup | Hard Fork, Last Week in AI |
| Business strategy | The Cognitive Revolution |
| Ethics and society | Your Undivided Attention, Eye on AI |
| Technical depth | Practical AI, Latent Space |

Start with Hard Fork (most accessible) or AI Breakdown (shortest). Add others based on your interests and available time.
