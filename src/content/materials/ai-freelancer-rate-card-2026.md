## How to Set Your Rate

**Step 1: Identify your experience level honestly.**
- **Beginner:** Less than 6 months using AI tools professionally. Can execute tasks but limited portfolio.
- **Intermediate:** 6-18 months experience. Strong portfolio. Can handle complex projects independently.
- **Expert:** 18+ months experience. Proven results with measurable ROI. Can consult on AI strategy.

**Step 2: Calculate the client's ROI.**
Your price should be 10-30% of the value you create. If your AI automation saves a client $50,000/year, charge $5,000-15,000 for setup.

**Step 3: Package, don't hourly.**
Never quote hourly rates for AI work. The value is in the deliverable. A $300 blog post written in 30 minutes is a $300 blog post — the speed is your competitive advantage, not a reason to discount.

**Step 4: Increase by 20% every 3 months.**
AI skills are in extreme demand. If clients accept your rate immediately, you are underpriced. Raise rates until 30% of prospects say no — that means you are priced correctly.

The AI freelance market rewards specialists over generalists. Pick one category from this rate card. Master it. Build a portfolio of 5-10 case studies. Then price at the intermediate level from day one — even if you feel like a beginner.

The market is too hot and demand too high to underprice yourself. Companies are desperate for people who can make AI work for their business. Be that person and charge what the work is worth.
