# AI Virtual Assistant Service: $2K-$6K/Month Managing Executives' Lives

Traditional virtual assistants charge $25-50/hour and handle 1-3 clients. AI-powered virtual assistants use automation tools to handle 5-10 clients, charging $500-1,500/month per executive. The AI handles email triage, scheduling, research, and travel booking while you manage relationships and handle complex requests.

## Why Executives Pay Premium for AI VAs

A full-time executive assistant costs $50,000-80,000/year. A fractional AI-powered VA costs $6,000-18,000/year — 70-85% less — and is available 24/7.

**What executives need managed:**
- **Email triage:** 100-300 emails/day sorted by priority, with draft responses for routine messages
- **Calendar management:** Meeting scheduling, conflict resolution, travel time buffers
- **Travel booking:** Flights, hotels, restaurants, ground transportation
- **Research:** Competitive intelligence, meeting prep, industry analysis
- **Expense reports:** Receipt collection, categorization, submission
- **Personal tasks:** Gift ordering, appointment scheduling, reservation management

AI handles 70-80% of these tasks. You handle the 20-30% that requires judgment, relationship awareness, and discretion.

## The AI Workflow

### Email Management (saves 2-3 hours/day per client)
1. AI scans all incoming email using Claude API
2. Categorizes: Urgent/Action Required/FYI/Spam
3. Drafts responses for routine emails (meeting confirmations, information requests)
4. Flags items needing the executive's personal attention
5. You review AI drafts, send approved ones, escalate complex items

### Calendar Management (saves 1 hour/day)
1. Calendly or Reclaim.ai handles scheduling requests automatically
2. AI optimizes the calendar — groups meetings, adds travel buffers, protects focus time
3. You handle rescheduling conflicts and VIP meeting coordination

### Research (saves 3-5 hours/week)
1. Executive asks: "Find me the top 5 AI companies in healthcare under $50M valuation"
2. You prompt Claude/Perplexity with the research brief
3. AI delivers structured research in minutes
4. You review, format, and deliver a polished brief
