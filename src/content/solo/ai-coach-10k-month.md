# AI Coaching & Training: How to Earn $5K-$15K/Month Teaching People AI

75% of professionals want to learn AI skills. Most have no idea where to start. Meanwhile, 58% of businesses are using AI but barely any of their employees have been trained. That disconnect is a huge business opportunity if you understand AI tools well enough to teach.

## The Opportunity

LinkedIn's 2025 Workforce Report ranked AI literacy as the #1 skill gap in the professional workforce. Companies are budgeting $1,000-5,000 per employee for AI training in 2026. Individuals are spending $100-500 on AI courses and coaching.

The problem on the supply side: there are not enough qualified trainers. Universities teach theory. YouTube covers the basics. What professionals and businesses actually need is practical, hands-on guidance for using AI in their specific role and industry. If you can deliver that, the market is wide open.

## Revenue Streams

### 1. One-on-One AI Coaching ($150-300/hour)

Help individuals master AI tools for their specific profession. A marketing manager learns to use Claude for campaign strategy. A real estate agent learns to use AI for listing descriptions and market analysis. A freelancer learns to build an AI-powered service business.

**Income potential:** 15-20 coaching sessions per week × $200/hour = $3,000-4,000/week = $12,000-16,000/month.

### 2. Corporate AI Workshops ($2,000-10,000/day)

Companies hire you to train their teams on AI tools. A typical workshop: 4-8 hours, 10-30 participants, hands-on exercises with ChatGPT/Claude tailored to the company's workflows.

**Income potential:** 2-4 workshops per month × $3,000-5,000 = $6,000-20,000/month.

### 3. Group Coaching Programs ($500-2,000/person)

Run a 4-6 week cohort program teaching AI skills to a group of 10-30 people. Weekly live sessions, homework assignments, community access.

**Income potential:** 1 cohort per month × 15 people × $1,000 = $15,000/month.

### 4. Online Course (Passive Income)

Record your best workshop content as an online course. Sell on Teachable, Kajabi, or Udemy for $97-497.

**Income potential:** $1,000-10,000/month depending on marketing.

## What to Teach

The highest-demand training topics:

| Topic | Audience | Rate |
|-------|----------|------|
| "AI for Your Marketing Team" | Marketing departments | $5,000/workshop |
| "ChatGPT for Sales Professionals" | Sales teams | $3,000-5,000/workshop |
| "AI Productivity for Executives" | C-suite | $5,000-10,000/workshop |
| "AI for Small Business Owners" | SMBs | $200-300/person group |
| "Build Your AI Side Hustle" | Career changers | $150-200/hr coaching |
| "AI for [Specific Industry]" | Vertical focus | Premium pricing |

## Getting Started

**Month 1:** Define your niche (which audience + which AI skills). Create a 2-hour workshop curriculum. Deliver it free to 3 groups for testimonials and practice.

**Month 2:** Launch paid workshops. Price: $1,500-2,000 for a half-day. Sell through LinkedIn, local business networks, and chamber of commerce events.

**Month 3-4:** Add 1-on-1 coaching at $200/hour. Build a waitlist from workshop attendees who want deeper support.

**Month 5-6:** Launch a group coaching cohort. Use workshop content as the foundation. Price: $1,000 per person for a 6-week program.

**Month 7+:** Create online course from your best material. This becomes a passive income stream while you continue live workshops and coaching.

## The Big Picture

You do not need a PhD for this. You need practical experience with AI tools and the ability to explain things clearly. The demand is enormous — 75% of professionals want training, pricing sits at $200-500/hour, and the market grows every time a new AI tool launches. If you are good with AI and can teach, $5,000-15,000/month is very achievable.
