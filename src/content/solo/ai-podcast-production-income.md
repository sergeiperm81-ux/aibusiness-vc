# AI Podcast Production: Earn $2K-$8K/Month as a Podcast Producer

There are 4.4 million podcasts globally, and 90% of them have terrible production quality — inconsistent uploads, no show notes, no clips, no promotion. AI tools cut podcast production time by 70%, enabling one person to produce 10-20 podcasts per month as a service. Charge $200-500 per episode and earn $2,000-8,000/month.

## Why Podcasters Pay for Production

Most podcasters are experts in their field (business, health, tech) but not in production. They record episodes but struggle with:

- Editing out ums, pauses, and mistakes (2-3 hours per episode manually)
- Writing show notes and descriptions (30-60 minutes)
- Creating promotional clips for social media (1-2 hours)
- Transcribing episodes for SEO (30 minutes to set up + review)
- Publishing across platforms (30 minutes)

**Total per episode without AI: 5-7 hours of post-production work.**

With AI tools, you do all of this in 45-90 minutes per episode.

## The AI Production Workflow

**Step 1: AI Editing (15-20 min)**
Descript ($24/month) automatically removes filler words ("um," "uh," "like"), silences, and false starts. It also generates a text transcript that you can edit like a Word document — deleting text deletes the audio. What took 2 hours now takes 15 minutes.

**Step 2: AI Show Notes (5-10 min)**
Feed the transcript to Claude. Prompt: "Generate podcast show notes with timestamps, key takeaways, and guest bio from this transcript." Review and polish. Done in 10 minutes instead of 45.

**Step 3: AI Social Clips (10-15 min)**
Opus Clip ($15/month) analyzes the full episode and automatically identifies the most engaging 30-60 second moments. It adds captions, formats for TikTok/Reels/Shorts, and generates 5-10 clips per episode. What took 2 hours now takes 15 minutes.

**Step 4: AI Transcription & SEO (5 min)**
Descript already generated the transcript. Format it for the podcast website. Add headers and timestamps. This makes the episode searchable by Google — most podcasters skip this, losing thousands of potential listeners.

**Step 5: Publishing (10 min)**
Upload to hosting platform, paste show notes, schedule social clips. Repeat for each client.

**Total per episode: 45-60 minutes.**
