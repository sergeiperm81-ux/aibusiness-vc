# AI Photo Editing Service: $1K-$5K/Month for E-Commerce and Real Estate

Professional photo editing costs $5-50 per image. AI tools do the same work in seconds for pennies. The gap creates a service opportunity: offer AI-powered photo editing to businesses at $2-15 per image, process hundreds per day, and earn $1,000-5,000/month. E-commerce sellers and real estate agents are the hungriest buyers.

## The Service

### E-Commerce Product Photography ($2-10/image)
- Background removal and replacement (white, lifestyle, contextual)
- Color correction and consistency across product lines
- Shadow and reflection addition
- Image resizing for Amazon, Shopify, Etsy requirements
- AI-generated lifestyle scenes showing products in use

**Volume:** A mid-size e-commerce store adds 50-200 new products monthly. At $5/image with 3 images per product = $750-3,000/month per client.

### Real Estate Photo Enhancement ($5-15/image)
- Virtual staging ($0.23-15/photo with AI vs $200-500 with physical staging)
- Sky replacement (gray sky → blue sky)
- Twilight conversion (day photos → dramatic evening shots)
- Lawn enhancement and decluttering
- Virtual renovation (show potential of outdated rooms)

**Volume:** A busy real estate agent lists 3-5 properties monthly with 20-30 photos each. At $10/image = $600-1,500/month per agent.

### Portrait Enhancement ($5-20/image)
- Professional headshot touch-up
- Background replacement
- Lighting correction
- Skin smoothing (tasteful, not overdone)
- Format optimization for LinkedIn, websites, and print

## Income Examples

| Client Type | Images/Month | Price/Image | Monthly Revenue |
|-------------|-------------|------------|----------------|
| 3 e-commerce stores | 500 | $5 | $2,500 |
| 5 real estate agents | 400 | $10 | $4,000 |

**Time required:** With AI doing the actual editing, your role is quality control and client communication. 500 images/month takes 20-30 hours (3-4 minutes per image for review and adjustment).

## Finding Clients

**E-commerce:** Browse Shopify stores and Amazon sellers with poor product photos. Send a before/after sample — edit one of their existing product images for free and show the improvement.

**Real estate:** Contact agents through local MLS directories. Offer a free virtual staging of one property. The before/after is so dramatic it sells itself.

**Photographers:** Partner with photographers who do not want to do editing. You handle post-processing at bulk rates.

## The Bottom Line

AI photo editing is a volume business with excellent margins. AI tools cost pennies per image while clients pay dollars. At 500-1,000 images per month across 3-8 clients, you earn $2,500-7,000/month working 20-30 hours. The work is straightforward, the tools are mature, and the demand from e-commerce and real estate is constant.
