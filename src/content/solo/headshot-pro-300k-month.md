# HeadshotPro: How One Developer Earns $300K/Month with AI Headshots

Danny Postma built HeadshotPro — an AI headshot generator — to $300,000 per month in revenue while working solo from Bali. No employees. No investors. No office. Just one developer, AI APIs, and a problem everyone has: the need for professional headshots without the hassle and cost of a photographer.

## The Business Model

HeadshotPro solves a specific, expensive problem. A professional headshot session costs $150-500 and requires scheduling a photographer, traveling to a studio, and waiting days for edited photos. HeadshotPro charges $29-59 per person, delivers results in 2 hours, and requires only uploading selfies.

**How it works:**
1. Customer uploads 8-12 casual selfies
2. AI trains a model on the customer's face (takes ~30 minutes)
3. AI generates 120+ professional headshots in different styles, outfits, and backgrounds
4. Customer downloads their favorites

**The economics:**
- Customer pays: $29-59
- AI compute cost: ~$2-5 per customer
- Gross margin: 85-95%
- At $300K/month revenue with 90% margins = ~$270K/month profit

## Why It Works

**Universal demand.** Everyone needs a professional headshot — for LinkedIn, corporate websites, resumes, and social media. The total addressable market is hundreds of millions of professionals worldwide.

**Price disruption.** $29 vs $200+ for a photographer. 10x cheaper makes it an impulse buy rather than a considered purchase.

**B2B expansion.** Companies buy team packages for all employees. One corporate deal can be $5,000-50,000. This is where the real scale comes from.

**Viral mechanics.** People share their AI headshots on social media. Every share is free marketing. LinkedIn posts showing "AI vs real headshot" comparisons go viral regularly.

## The Solo Founder Playbook

Danny Postma is a serial solo builder. Before HeadshotPro, he built and sold multiple smaller AI products. His approach:

1. **Find a specific pain point** that AI can solve 10x cheaper than the current solution
2. **Build fast** — ship an MVP in weeks, not months
3. **Price for volume** — $29-59 makes it accessible to everyone
4. **Automate everything** — AI handles the product, Stripe handles payments, email handles support
5. **Stay solo** — no employees means near-100% margins

## What Solopreneurs Can Learn

HeadshotPro proves a specific formula: **take expensive human service → replace with AI → charge 10% of the original price → serve 100x more customers.**

Other opportunities following the same pattern:

| Human Service | Cost | AI Version | AI Price |
|--------------|------|-----------|----------|
| Professional headshot | $200-500 | AI headshot | $29-59 |
| Logo design | $500-2,000 | AI logo | $29-99 |
| Product photography | $50-200/image | AI product shots | $5-15/image |
| Resume writing | $200-500 | AI resume | $19-49 |
| Voice acting | $500-5,000 | AI voice | $5-50 |

Each of these can be built by a solo founder with existing AI tools (Stable Diffusion, Flux, ElevenLabs) and standard web infrastructure.

## The Bottom Line

$300K/month as a solo founder with no employees and no investors. HeadshotPro is proof that the AI gold rush rewards people who find specific expensive problems and solve them cheaply with AI. The barriers to entry have never been lower — the APIs exist, the infrastructure is commodity, and the demand is real.
