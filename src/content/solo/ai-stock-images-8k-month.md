# Selling AI-Generated Stock Images: $3K-$8K/Month in Passive Income

One documented creator earns $3,000-8,000 per month selling AI-generated images on stock photography sites, licensing 2,000-3,000 images monthly. The income is largely passive — after the initial creation and upload phase, images sell repeatedly with zero additional work.

## How It Works

**Step 1: Generate.** Use Midjourney ($10-30/month), DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion to create high-quality images that businesses actually need. Not art — commercial imagery.

**Step 2: Curate.** Not every AI image is sellable. Select the best 10-20% that look professional, have clean compositions, and serve clear commercial purposes.

**Step 3: Upload.** Submit to stock platforms (Adobe Stock, Shutterstock, iStock, Pond5). Add keywords, titles, descriptions optimized for search.

**Step 4: Earn.** Every time someone downloads your image, you earn a royalty. Prices range from $0.25 to $15+ per download depending on the license type and platform.

## What Sells

The highest-earning categories for AI-generated stock images:

| Category | Why It Sells | Avg Downloads/Image |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Business/Corporate | Every presentation, website, brochure needs these | 50-200/year |
| Technology/AI | Ironic but true — AI articles need AI imagery | 100-500/year |
| Abstract Backgrounds | Used in design, presentations, apps | 200-1000/year |
| Nature/Landscapes | Always in demand | 50-150/year |
| Food/Lifestyle | Marketing, social media | 30-100/year |
| Textures/Patterns | Design and development | 100-300/year |

**The winners:** Abstract tech backgrounds, diverse business scenes, and clean conceptual images earn the most. These are images that are expensive to photograph traditionally but trivial to generate with AI.

## Platform Comparison

| Platform | Commission | AI Policy | Monthly Payout |
|----------|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| Adobe Stock | 33% | Accepts AI (must disclose) | $1+ per download |
| Shutterstock | 15-40% | Accepts AI (must disclose) | $0.25-2+ per download |
| iStock | 15-45% | Accepts AI with restrictions | Varies |
| Pond5 | 40-60% | Accepts AI | Higher per-download |
| Wirestock | Distributes to multiple | Accepts AI | Aggregated |

**Best strategy:** Use Wirestock ($0/month) to distribute to multiple platforms simultaneously. Or focus on Adobe Stock which has the highest per-download payouts and the clearest AI acceptance policy.

## Getting Started

**Month 1:** Generate 500 images across 5-10 commercial categories. Upload to 2-3 platforms. Cost: $30/month for Midjourney + your time.

**Month 2-3:** Analyze which categories get the most downloads. Double down on winners. Upload 300-500 more images.

**Month 4-6:** Portfolio reaches 2,000-3,000 images. Monthly income hits $500-2,000.

**Month 7-12:** Portfolio at 5,000+ images. Monthly income stabilizes at $2,000-5,000. New uploads add incremental growth.

**Year 2:** Maintenance mode. Upload 100-200 images per month to keep growing. Income: $3,000-8,000/month with minimal ongoing effort.

## Legal Considerations

- **Disclosure required:** Most platforms require you to tag images as AI-generated
- **No copyrightable output:** Purely AI-generated images may not be copyrightable in the US. However, stock platforms still sell licenses for their use.
- **No faces of real people:** AI-generated photorealistic faces can cause legal issues. Stick to scenes, objects, and abstract imagery.
- **Platform terms change:** Stay updated on each platform's AI policy. Adobe and Shutterstock are currently the most AI-friendly.

## The Bottom Line

AI stock images represent one of the truest forms of passive income in the AI economy. After the initial effort of generating and uploading a portfolio of 5,000+ images, the income continues with minimal maintenance. $3,000-8,000/month is achievable within 12 months. The barrier to entry is low — $30/month for Midjourney and a few hours per week of generation and curation.
