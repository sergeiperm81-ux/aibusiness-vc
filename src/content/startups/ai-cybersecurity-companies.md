AI can probe network vulnerabilities thousands of times faster than human hackers. The only way to defend against AI-powered attacks at machine speed is with AI-powered defense at machine speed.

## How AI Protects Companies

### Threat Detection (Finding Attacks in Real-Time)
AI analyzes billions of network events per day, identifying patterns that indicate attacks. A human analyst reviewing 10,000 alerts per day catches maybe 20% of real threats. AI catches 95%+ while generating 80% fewer false positives.

### Automated Response (Stopping Attacks in Milliseconds)
When AI detects a threat, it can automatically isolate affected systems, block malicious traffic, and initiate remediation — all before a human analyst even sees the alert. Response time drops from hours to milliseconds.

### Predictive Security (Stopping Attacks Before They Happen)
AI models predict which vulnerabilities attackers are most likely to exploit based on threat intelligence, dark web monitoring, and attack pattern analysis. Companies patch the most critical vulnerabilities first instead of treating all equally.
