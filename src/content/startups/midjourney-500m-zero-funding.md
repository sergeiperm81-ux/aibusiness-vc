# Midjourney: $500M Revenue, Zero Funding, 40 Employees

Midjourney does roughly $500 million in annual revenue with 40 people and zero outside money. That's $12.5 million per employee — a ratio that makes even the most efficient tech companies look bloated. It's arguably the most impressive business in the entire AI industry.

## The Numbers That Matter

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Annual revenue | ~$500M |
| Employees | ~40 |
| Revenue per employee | $12.5M |
| External funding raised | $0 |
| Subscribers | 16M+ |
| Average revenue per user | ~$31/month |
| Estimated profit margin | 70-80% |
| Estimated annual profit | $350-400M |

For context: OpenAI has 3,000+ people generating $13B ($4.3M per employee). Anthropic has 1,200+ generating $5B ($4.2M per employee). Midjourney generates 3x more revenue per person than both AI giants.

## How They Make Money

Midjourney runs exclusively on Discord with dead-simple subscriptions:

- **Basic Plan:** $10/month — 200 image generations
- **Standard Plan:** $30/month — 15 hours of fast generation
- **Pro Plan:** $60/month — 30 hours of fast generation + stealth mode
- **Mega Plan:** $120/month — 60 hours of fast generation

No free tier. No freemium. No ads. Just subscriptions. That simplicity is part of what makes it work — no sales team, no enterprise contracts, no messy pricing negotiations.

## Why This Is Extraordinary

**Zero dilution.** Founder David Holz owns the whole company. At a conservative 10x revenue multiple, Midjourney is worth $5 billion+. He didn't give up a single percentage point. In an era where AI founders raise billions and get diluted to single-digit ownership, that's remarkable.

**Profitable from the start.** OpenAI lost $5 billion in 2024. Anthropic burns capital at staggering rates. Midjourney has been profitable since launch. That estimated $350-400M annual profit funds all R&D internally.

**No sales team needed.** The product sells itself. Sixteen million subscribers found Midjourney through word of mouth, social media, and the quality of the images. Zero salespeople. Effectively zero marketing budget.

## What AI Entrepreneurs Can Take Away

**1. You don't need to raise money.** The most profitable AI company raised $0. VC is a tool, not a requirement.

**2. Simple pricing wins.** Four tiers, no enterprise negotiation, no custom deals. Every customer pays the same way.

**3. Quality is the marketing.** Midjourney's images are so distinctive that every shared image is a billboard. The product IS the marketing.

**4. Stay small on purpose.** 40 people doing $500M means every person is essential and very well paid. No bureaucracy, no middle management, no waste.

**5. Consumers will pay.** While most AI startups chase enterprise contracts, Midjourney proved that millions of individuals happily pay $10-120/month for AI tools that make them more creative.

## The Upshot
Midjourney is proof that the best AI business isn't always the one raising the most money — it's the one making the most money per person, per dollar, per decision. $500M in revenue. 40 people. Zero funding. That's what an efficiently run AI company actually looks like.
