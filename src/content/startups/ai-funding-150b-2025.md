# $150 Billion in AI Funding in 2025: Where the Money Went

AI startups pulled in nearly $150 billion in 2025 — more than 40% of all global venture capital. This isn't gradual growth. It's a flood. Total VC globally was $425 billion across 24,000 companies, and AI grabbed a wildly outsized share. Here's where every dollar landed.

## The Mega-Rounds

The biggest checks went to foundation model companies building core AI infrastructure:

| Company | Amount | Valuation | Lead Investor |
|---------|--------|-----------|---------------|
| OpenAI | $40B | $300B+ | SoftBank |
| Scale AI | $14.3B | — | Meta |
| Anthropic | $13B | $183B | Multiple |
| Project Prometheus | $6.2B | — | — |
| xAI | $5.3B | $50B | — |
| Databricks | $4B+ | $134B | — |

Those six rounds alone: $82.8 billion. That's over 55% of all AI funding concentrated in half a dozen companies.

## Funding by Stage

| Stage | Share of AI Funding | Median Round Size |
|-------|-------------------|------------------|
| Growth/Late Stage | 66% (~$99B) | $200M+ |
| Series B | 15% (~$22.5B) | $45M |
| Series A | 12% (~$18B) | $18M |
| Seed | 5% (~$7.5B) | $4.2M |
| Pre-Seed | 2% (~$3B) | $1.5M |

Late-stage dominance makes sense — training frontier models is insanely capital-intensive. But the seed and Series A numbers show healthy deal flow. $25.5 billion went to early-stage companies, which means there's real opportunity for new entrants.

## What This Means for Different Players

**For founders:** There's never been more money sloshing around for AI startups. But the bar is higher than 2023 — investors want revenue, not demos. The median seed-stage AI startup now shows $10K-50K MRR before raising.

**For solo builders:** The mega-rounds go to foundation model companies, but that $25.5B in early-stage funding feeds an ecosystem of tools, platforms, and APIs that solo builders use cheap or free. Every dollar invested in AI infrastructure makes your AI product cheaper to build.

**For enterprises:** The flood of investment means more AI tools, better quality, lower prices. AI vendors are fighting hard for your business — use that use.

**For job seekers:** Companies flush with AI funding are hiring fast. The 55 US AI startups that raised $100M+ in 2025 collectively created thousands of high-paying roles. AI engineer salaries average $206,000.

## The 2026 Trajectory

February 2026 alone saw $189 billion in total startup funding — a single-month record — driven by massive AI rounds like OpenAI's $110 billion raise and Anthropic's $30 billion. The pace isn't slowing. It's speeding up.

## Where This Is Headed
$150 billion in AI funding in one year means something straightforward: the world's savviest investors are making the biggest bet in technology history on AI. Whether you're building, investing, or earning with AI — this capital wave creates opportunity at every level.
